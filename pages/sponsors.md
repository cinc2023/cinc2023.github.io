The Computing in Cardiology (CinC) conference dates back to 1974 and has been held around the globe in countries including Czech Republic, Italy, Singapore, Netherlands and France. For the first time since 2014, the CinC conference will be held in the United States, hosted by Emory University, the Morehouse School of Medicine, and the Georgia Institute of Technology in Atlanta, Georgia. The conference dates are **24th - 27th September 2023**.
